Escape Amnesia Manor is a dark, thrilling, and thought provoking 2D escape the room game. As with most escape the room games, the player will follow a breadcrumb trail of clues that leads to the discovery of riddles, keys, codes, and eventually, the unlocking of the final door to freedom. Escape Amnesia Manor has a strong emphasis on writing and world design, forcing you to think outside the room. Will you make it out and reach the goal?

As Lead Designer, I created the workings of Eascape Amnesia Manor. I wrote the story and designed gameplay.

I worked as a programmer for part of the project also. I helped to implement the drawing of objects to the screen.

I was the artist on hand for the project. With great dirrection from the art dirrector I was able to nail the style and mood.

I created sounds and music to immerse the player in the world. the spooky vibe is set immediatly with a classic organ chord.

Blast Some Rocks!

Originally created under the codename FOD20, GotOW is a total conversion/ expansion of Wizards of the Coast's Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition. The system was built from the ground up to bring Bethesda Game Studio's Fallout franchise to the table top roleplaying game genre. Its still in development, but you are welcome to take an early sneak peek

I worked tirelessly to create a rich apocalyptic experience based on Fallout in the D&D 5th Edition SRD. I reworked Fallouts perk system to work more like 5e Feats and reworked the way characters interact with the world via Skills

I am in the process of laying out my content into a professional quality PDF complete with custom art, graphics, and a whitty narrator. I am using Adobe InDesign to layout GotOW

I Must Investigate!

In Project IPRE a partner and I created a 2D game in the format of a non traditional shoot 'em up game with heavy influences from space and sci-fi genres. The player is piloting a crew through the cosmos and must fight to outrun The Hunger before it consumes The Starblaster and ends thier research of the great beyond.

We chose to design this game as "a shoot'em up without the shooting." Making it more of a runner. we choose this to stay faithful to the source material, The McElroy Brother's, The Adventure Zone: Balance

I worked to create the livly enemy we know as The Hunger. I also implemented the alert system and score managment for the game

I created the art for The Starblaster, Light of Creation, and Lucretia's shield. I also oversaw the UI style choices and overall visual design of the game

I recreated Griffin McElroy's original song, "The Starblaster", which was used during the final arch or The Adventure Zone. I wanted to pay respects to Griffin's story and music while also expanding my experience in digital audio creation
