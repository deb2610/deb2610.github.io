About Me

My name is Declan Behan. I am a 2nd year Game Design & Development student attending Rochester Institute of Technology. I am pasionate about front end design, UI/UX, designing games, writing Dungeons' & Dragons adventures, and producing music. I started my gaming journey in my small hometown of Jim Thorpe, Pennsylvania. I was launched into the virtual world of games with my trusty Nintendo DS and a twinkle in my eye. Now I am working towards my own D&D module, working on my second original soundtrack, and a 2D platformer about a boy and an umbrella.
